Poetry Books

The Sun and Her Flowers

Author: Rupi Kaur

Review :This is one of the books which one can read untold number of times. Rupi Kaur brings out all the preeminent emotions that we all living beings go through in our lives at some point or the other. The strong suit of her writing is that she expresses these otherwise compound emotions in the most discernible way anyone can ever do! This book is divided into five phases of emotions which are aptly compared to the Sunflowers as they wilt, fall, root, rise and bloom.

Leaves of Grass

Author :Walt Whitman

Review : Leaves of Grass is a rolling cascade of language that entrances with its steady rhythm. It is an impossibly ambitious attempt to capture the American world in ballad that attempts the impossible. Walt Whitman attempts to capture the world he is surrounded by and we get glimpses of hills and people and rivers and deserts and fields and workers as he explores it. It becomes, after a while, a rollicking ride through the recesses of consciousness.
